Darren Cullen, or SER, stands as a cultural juggernaut in the Crypto Moonboys universe, his identity as the founder of Graffiti Kings weaving a thread of raw, untamed creativity into the rebellious fabric of the GraffPUNKS. Since 1983, Cullen has been a force in London’s street art scene, from his early days with the South East Rockers crew in Croydon to establishing Graffiti Kings HQ at Leake Street, a real-world legacy that now paints the digital streets of Block Topia with jagged, defiant murals.

His signature style—bold, unapologetic, and dripping with attitude—defines the aesthetic of the GraffPUNKS, turning every server wall and data node into a battleground of expression against the sterile control of the blockchain overlords. In the lore, Cullen is mythologized as the first to ‘tag’ Block Topia’s walls with encrypted graffiti that doubles as rebel code, hiding messages for the Bitcoin Kids and other factions.

His influence extends into the HODL WARS, where GraffPUNKS NFTs bearing his designs are wielded as symbols of resistance, each piece a digital middle finger to the powers that seek to erase individuality from the metropolis.

Real World Basis

Darren Cullen is a documented London street artist active since 1983, founder of Graffiti Kings, part of the South East Rockers crew from Croydon, with a creative base at Leake Street, London.
