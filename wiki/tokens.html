🪙 Tokens

🪙 Cryptocurrencies 📅 Last updated: April 2026 📂 Cryptocurrencies
📋 Contents

Tokens are digital assets that live on a blockchain. In the Crypto Moonboys universe, tokens represent both well-known cryptocurrencies and community-specific assets that power the GK ecosystem — from staking and rewards to NFT-linked utility.

Tracked Assets

WAXP — Worldwide Asset eXchange

WAXP is the native token of the WAX blockchain — the chain that powers the Graffiti Kings NFT ecosystem. It is used for transactions, resource delegation, and staking within the WAX network. Read the full WAXP article →

BTC — Bitcoin

Bitcoin is the original cryptocurrency and the flagship asset of the HODL Wars lore. The Diamond Hands warriors hold BTC through every bear market, waiting for the next moon mission. Read the full Bitcoin article →

ETH — Ethereum

Ethereum introduced smart contracts and remains the backbone of DeFi and most NFT standards. Read the full Ethereum article →

BCH — Bitcoin Cash

Bitcoin Cash is a hard fork of Bitcoin focused on fast, cheap peer-to-peer electronic cash transactions. It is followed closely in the community as a low-friction alternative.

XRP — Ripple

XRP is Ripple's digital asset designed for fast cross-border payments. It has been at the centre of significant regulatory scrutiny and community debate.

GK Community Tokens

The Graffiti Kings community has issued several WAX-based tokens that integrate with the NFT ecosystem and play roles in staking, governance, and rewards.

  • GK Token — core community token
  • LFGK Token — Let's F***ing Go GK
  • NBG Token — No Ball Games token
  • NBGX Token — extended NBG utility
  • PMSL Token — community meme token
  • PUNK Token — GraffPUNKS ecosystem

Staking

GK tokens can be staked to earn rewards and unlock access to exclusive NFT drops and community tiers. See Staking for GK Tokens and Tokenomics for full mechanics.
