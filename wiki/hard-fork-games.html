🎭 Hard Fork Games

🎭 Lore 📅 Last updated: 2026-03-28 📂 Lore

The Hard Fork Games’ most infamous trial, the 'Consensus Fracture,' forces competitors to split into opposing teams mid-battle, simulating a blockchain fork by altering the arena’s rules in real-time, with the losing side often erased from the match’s digital record as if they never existed.

Known Facts

Competitive event for elite status

Hosted by Queen Sarah P-fly

Grants HODL X Warriors title

The Hard Fork Games’ most infamous trial, the 'Consensus Fracture,' forces competitors to split into opposing teams mid-battle, simulating a blockchain fork by altering the arena’s rules in real-time, with the losing side often erased from the match’s digital record as if they never existed.

Queen Sarah P-fly is said to infuse the Games with her own coded essence, embedding hidden 'Royal Directives' within the challenges that, if deciphered, grant competitors secret advantages, though failure to obey these unseen commands can lead to catastrophic penalties.

The title of HODL X Warriors is not just honorary; it comes with a permanent 'HODL Mark,' a blockchain tattoo that enhances the bearer’s stats in all GraffPUNKS games and events, a visible sign of their elite status that inspires both reverence and envy.
