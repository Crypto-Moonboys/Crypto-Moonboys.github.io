⚙️ Darren Cullen (SER) - Real

⚙️ Concepts 📅 Last updated: March 2026 📂 Concepts

Founder and artist of Graffiti Kings, visionary behind Crypto Moonboys.

Faction

Allegiance: GRAFFPUNKS/GKniftyHEADS

Known Facts

  • Founder of Graffiti Kings since 1983.
  • Based in Croydon/South-East London with HQ on Leake Street.
  • Painted sets for London 2012 Olympic opening ceremony.
  • Created Kate Middleton hyperrealist mural in Kent (2013).
  • Collaborated with Adidas, Nike, Calvin Klein, The X Factor, The Apprentice.
  • Founded Graffiti Queens in Decentraland (April 2021, largest all-female NFT exhibition).

Lore

Real-world founder whose influence extends into lore as a character representing street art rebellion.

Real World Basis

Active since 1983 in Croydon/South-East London, Graffiti Kings HQ on Leake Street, painted sets for London 2012 Olympic opening ceremony, created Kate Middleton mural in Kent (2013), collaborated with major brands, founded Graffiti Queens in Decentraland.
