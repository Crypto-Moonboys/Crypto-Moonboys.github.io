🎭 XRP KIDs

🎭 Lore 📅 Last updated: 2026-03-28 📂 Lore

During the chaos of the Triple Fork Event, several XRP KIDs were reportedly altered by the blockchain fracture, gaining unique visual glitches or enhanced in-game traits tied to the forked realities, making them even rarer and more valuable. Known as 'Forked Kids' among collectors, these altered NFTs are believed to resonate with the event’s Triad Surge, with holders claiming temporary boosts in Battlemech Blast matches, fueling speculation that they are blessed—or cursed—by the fork’s chaotic energy.

XRP KIDs, a genesis NFT collection of 100 hand-crafted pieces by Charlie Buster on the XRPL blockchain, captures the spirit of escaped Bitcoin Kids, aligning with the HODL Warriors’ relentless fight for freedom in Block Topia. Each NFT serves as a digital relic of rebellion, embodying the raw defiance of youth against centralized oppression, with holders often seen as torchbearers of Alfie 'The Bitcoin Kid' Blaze’s enduring legacy.

Beyond their collectible status, XRP KIDs act as narrative keys within the Crypto Moonboys universe, unlocking exclusive rewards, events, and hidden storylines tied to HODL WARS conflicts, often granting holders unique advantages in staking battles or access to restricted arenas. Their integration into gameplay has made them targets for espionage, with rival factions like the Street Kingdoms plotting heists to steal these powerful artifacts from HODL Warrior strongholds.

A persistent rumor among Block Topia’s warriors claims that certain XRP KIDs are embedded with 'Blaze Codes,' hidden data fragments tied to Alfie 'The Bitcoin Kid' Blaze’s original escape from centralized control, potentially revealing locations of forgotten digital caches or summoning long-lost allies. Though unconfirmed, this legend has driven a frenzy of trades and duels, with holders guarding their NFTs fiercely against hackers and thieves seeking to crack the mythical codes.
