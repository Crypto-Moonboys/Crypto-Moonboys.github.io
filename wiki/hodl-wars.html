THE CRYPTO MOONBOYS — HODL WARS LORE$

🌙 Community Lore 📅 Last updated: March 2026 📂 Lore
📋 Contents

The HODL Wars is the central mythological lore of the Crypto Moonboys community — an ongoing saga of battles between the forces of Diamond Hands[1] and Paper Hands[2], fought across the volatile battlegrounds of the crypto markets. In this universe, every market dip is a siege, every rug pull a betrayal, and every moon mission a triumphant crusade.

The Lore Universe

The HODL Wars universe is a mythological realm where crypto traders are reimagined as warriors locked in an eternal struggle for financial sovereignty. The battleground is the market itself — charts replace maps, price action replaces troop movements, and every wallet address is a fortress.

At the heart of the universe stand the HODL Warriors — the true believers who hold through every crash. Above them loom the mysterious Whale Lords, ancient powers who move markets with single transactions. And lurking at the edges are those destined for the WAGMI Prophecy — the ultimate promise that all true holders will one day reach the moon.

The lore draws from real crypto culture: the slang, the memes, the legendary price events, and the community rituals that define life on Crypto Twitter, Reddit, and Discord servers worldwide. It transforms lived experience into epic mythology.

Origins: The Satoshi Scroll

The HODL Wars lore traces its origins to The Satoshi Scroll — the mythologised form of the original Bitcoin Whitepaper[4], handed down by the enigmatic Satoshi Nakamoto. The Scroll is said to contain the foundational laws of the crypto revolution: decentralisation, self-custody, and the sacred art of HODLing.

"Not your keys, not your coins — this is the first law of the HODL Wars."[3]

The original HODL battle cry itself was born in a now-legendary BitcoinTalk forum post from 2013, where a passionate Bitcoin holder — misspelling "hold" in a moment of market panic — accidentally coined the war cry that would define a generation of crypto warriors. The Scroll enshrined this cry as doctrine.

The Factions

Three great factions divide the HODL Wars universe:

Diamond Hands — The true believers. Warriors blessed with the strength to hold through any storm. They are impervious to FUD, immune to panic, and march always toward the moon. Diamond Hands warriors are the backbone of the HODL army and the heroes of the lore.

Paper Hands — The capitulators. Those who sell at the first sign of trouble, trading long-term gains for short-term relief. In the lore, Paper Hands warriors are not villains but tragic figures — souls seduced by fear, documented in cautionary chronicles, always one redemption arc away from becoming Diamond Hands.

Whale Lords — The ancient powers. Market-moving entities who hold thousands of BTC and ETH, whose every transaction reshapes the battlefield. They are neither heroes nor villains — they are forces of nature, above the war yet shaping its every outcome.

The Great Battles

The HODL Wars are punctuated by three classes of great conflicts:

The Great Dip — The recurring cataclysm. Sudden market crashes that strike without warning, separating Diamond Hands from Paper Hands in moments of extreme pressure. Every Great Dip is a test of faith; every survivor emerges stronger.

The Bear Market Siege — The prolonged dark chapter. Where The Great Dip is a lightning strike, The Bear Market Siege is a grinding war of attrition — months or years of declining prices that exhaust even the most committed warriors. The Siege of 2018 and the Siege of 2022 are inscribed in the chronicles as the two greatest tests of the HODL age.

The Rug Pull Wars — The wars of betrayal. When unscrupulous developer factions abandon their projects, fleeing with investor funds in the dark of night. These are the treacherous conflicts that gave birth to the HODL Warriors' most sacred principle: DYOR (Do Your Own Research).

The FOMO Plague

No account of the HODL Wars is complete without acknowledging the great epidemic that afflicts warriors on both sides: The FOMO Plague. Fear Of Missing Out spreads through social media like a digital contagion, turning rational traders into panic-buyers and transforming Diamond Hands into Paper Hands in the span of a single viral tweet.

Those who succumb to the FOMO Plague invariably end up in the NGMI Chronicles — the cautionary records of warriors who bought at the top, sold at the bottom, and bore witness to their own financial undoing. The Chronicles are studied by all new warriors as essential field intelligence.

The Moon Mission

At the heart of every HODL Warrior's quest lies The Moon Mission — the prophesied journey to infinite gains. Foretold in The Satoshi Scroll and confirmed by The WAGMI Prophecy, the Moon Mission promises that all who hold with Diamond Hands through every dip, every siege, and every rug pull will eventually ascend to the moon.

"When Lambo?" is the sacred question asked by all Moon Mission participants — a ritual affirmation of faith in the inevitable arrival of generational wealth. The Moon Mission is not merely a financial goal; it is a spiritual journey, a test of character, and the ultimate validation of the HODL philosophy.

The WAGMI Prophecy — "We're All Gonna Make It" — is the battle cry of the Moon Mission. It is the antidote to NGMI, the light at the end of every Bear Market Siege, and the promise that binds the community together across bull runs and crypto winters alike.
