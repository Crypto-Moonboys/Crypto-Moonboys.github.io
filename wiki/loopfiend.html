His temporal distortions have been known to trap entire factions in repeating cycles of defeat, forcing warriors to relive their last moments while he vanishes into the next timeline, untouchable and unseen.

His temporal distortions often emerge in high-stakes events like the Hard Fork Games, granting advantages or sowing chaos by freezing critical decisions in endless loops or fast-forwarding through defenses. Accounts from survivors describe Loopfiend’s interventions as nightmarish, with battles restarting mid-strike or enemies vanishing into skipped frames, his unknown allegiance casting uncertainty over whether he aids rebellion or undermines it with every tick of warped time.

Rumors persist in Block Topia’s data archives that Loopfiend is tethered to a shattered node of the Sacred Chain, a fractured timestamp that grants him dominion over the metaverse’s chronology. Whether he seeks to repair this broken link or exploit it to unravel the fabric of reality itself is a question no faction can answer, but his flickering presence at the edge of every conflict warns of a power that could rewrite history—or erase it entirely.
