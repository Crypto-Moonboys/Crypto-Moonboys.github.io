📅 Great Datapocalypse of 2789

📅 Lore 📅 Last updated: March 2026 📂 Lore

Cataclysmic event in the lore leading to the creation of GKniftyHEADS and Block Topia.

Faction

Allegiance: None (Historical Event)

Known Facts

  • Cataclysmic event in 2789 leading to the creation of Block Topia.
  • Genesis Kernel uploaded billions of minds into a decentralized quantum network.
  • Resulted in the creation of spectral heads of consciousness, the GKniftyHEADS.

Lore

The Great Datapocalypse of 2789 is a foundational event in the Crypto Moonboys universe, resulting in the Genesis Kernel uploading billions of minds into a decentralized quantum network, creating the GKniftyHEADS and Block Topia.
