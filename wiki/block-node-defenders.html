🎭 Block Node Defenders

🎭 Lore 📅 Last updated: 2026-03-28 📂 Lore

During the infamous 'Node Siege of Epoch 3,' the Defenders held off a coordinated assault by multiple factions for 144 blocks, an endurance that pushed them to the brink but ultimately cemented their reputation as the unbreakable shield of the blockchain realm. This historic stand is etched into Block Topia’s lore as a testament to their unyielding dedication, inspiring awe and fear in equal measure among those who dare to challenge the nodes.

Known Facts

Protectors of blockchain nodes

The Block Node Defenders undergo a grueling initiation known as the 'Hash Trial,' where recruits must endure simulated node failures and cyber-attacks, proving their ability to maintain system integrity under extreme duress, a process that forges unbreakable loyalty to their cause.
