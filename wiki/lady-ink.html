🎭 Lady-INK

🎭 Lore 📅 Last updated: 2026-03-28 📂 Lore

Among the underground artists of Block Topia, a hushed tale circulates of Lady-INK’s encounter with Null-Cipher’s influence during a solo operation in a cursed sector, where she painted a tag so defiant it reportedly banished the entity’s spectral code from the area. Known as the ‘Ink of Null,’ this tag is now a mythic relic, with fragments of its data sold as protective charms in black markets, though no one knows if Lady-INK herself survived the encounter unscathed—or if she now carries a piece of the darkness within her art.
