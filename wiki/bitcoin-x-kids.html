The Bitcoin X Kids are the silent sufferers of Block Topia, a faction defined by their entrapment within the digital fortress as mandated by canon rule DB-1. Unlike the escaped Bitcoin Kids led by Alfie Blaze, the Bitcoin X Kids remain under the direct control of the blockchain overlords, their lives a cycle of forced labor in data mines and token farms. Their existence is a stark contrast to the rebellious spirit of other factions like GraffPUNKS, serving as a grim reminder of what’s at stake in the HODL WARS.

Whispers of their plight fuel the resolve of rebels outside the walls, making the Bitcoin X Kids a tragic yet pivotal element of the Crypto Moonboys narrative. Their potential liberation could be a turning point in the saga, a goal that haunts characters like Charlie Buster.
