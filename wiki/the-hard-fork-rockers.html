The HARD FORK ROCKERS are the rebels of blockchain splits, their very existence a protest against the suffocating grip of centralized powers, as they transform the pain of digital fractures into a rallying cry for freedom, drawing strength from the chaos of forks to fuel their endless war on conformity.
