⚔️ Lore 📅 Last updated: March 2026 📂 Lore

Masters of trickery, they lure rivals into deadly traps with playful NFT decoys and avatar disguises, turning urban skirmishes into chaotic games where laughter often precedes destruction. Their playful demeanor hides a penchant for deception that has felled countless enemies in the neon-lit alleys of the city.

They are the wildcard of Block Topia’s conflicts, their alliances as fleeting as their traps are deadly, making them a faction that no one can truly predict or trust. To underestimate them is to invite ruin, as their games are always played for keeps.
