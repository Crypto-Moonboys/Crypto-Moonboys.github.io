PUNK Coin is a documented entity inside the Crypto Moonboys lore system.

Known Facts

PUNK Coin was discovered as the security token of the GraffPUNKS network, a vital asset in the Crypto Moonboys universe securing Block Topia’s multi-chain economy on the WAX blockchain, often tied to the GKniftyHEADS’ cultural influence. Its role in protecting transactions across platforms like WAXP, Bitcoin Cash, Solana, and XRPL underscores its importance, embodying the punk spirit of rebellion while fortifying the metaverse against threats, a digital shield in games like HODL WARS.
