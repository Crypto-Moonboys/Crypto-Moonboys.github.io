The WAGMI Prophecy

🌙 Community Lore 📅 Last updated: March 2026 📂 Lore
📋 Contents

The WAGMI Prophecy (We're All Gonna Make It) is the ultimate optimistic declaration of the HODL Wars — the belief that all true HODL Warriors with Diamond Hands will eventually achieve the Moon Mission. It is the antidote to the NGMI Chronicles and the guiding star through every Bear Market Siege.

Origins of WAGMI

WAGMI originated on Crypto Twitter[1] as a response to the NGMI cultural pattern — a collective counter-narrative to the doom and FUD that characterised bear market discourse. Where NGMI judged and condemned, WAGMI affirmed and invited.

The phrase gained extraordinary cultural weight during the Bored Ape Yacht Club and broader NFT era of 2021[2], when it became the universal salutation between creators, collectors, and community members — a password, a promise, and a philosophy compressed into five letters. Its use spread beyond NFTs to encompass all crypto culture, and eventually into mainstream cultural discourse.

In the HODL Wars lore, WAGMI is not merely a phrase but a prophetic declaration inscribed in the final chapter of The Satoshi Scroll — a promise made by the architects of decentralisation to all who would follow the path of Diamond Hands.

The Prophecy Text

The sacred declaration of the WAGMI Prophecy, as recorded in the chronicles:

"WAGMI. Diamond Hands forever. The moon awaits all true believers."

These three sentences encapsulate the entire philosophy of the HODL Wars in distilled form: the collective affirmation (WAGMI), the personal commitment (Diamond Hands forever), and the promised destination (the moon awaits). Every HODL Warrior carries these words as their primary article of faith.

WAGMI vs NGMI

The WAGMI / NGMI dichotomy is the HODL Wars' central philosophical tension — the eternal question of whether any given warrior will achieve the Moon Mission or be recorded in the NGMI Chronicles.

Crucially, the lore maintains that the distinction is not permanent. Warriors oscillate between WAGMI and NGMI states throughout their journey, and the Chronicles are full of redemption arcs. WAGMI is not a birthright but an achievement — earned through Diamond Hands, DYOR, and the willingness to learn from every loss.

Community Declarations

In community practice, WAGMI serves multiple ritual functions: it is used as a greeting, a rallying cry during bear markets, a sign-off on bullish analyses, and a declaration of solidarity during difficult market periods. When a whale dumps and red candles appear, "WAGMI" in the group chat is simultaneously a joke, a comfort, and a genuine reaffirmation of long-term conviction.

The community's use of WAGMI has evolved to include ironic and self-aware iterations — "definitely WAGMI," "WAGMI (probably)" — that acknowledge the uncertainty of the path while maintaining the orientation toward eventual success. This layered use reflects the maturity of the community: experienced warriors hold conviction and humility simultaneously.
