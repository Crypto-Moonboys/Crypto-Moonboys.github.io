Moon Mission

🌙 Community Lore 📅 Last updated: March 2026 📂 Lore
📋 Contents

The Moon Mission is the ultimate goal of every HODL Warrior — the prophesied journey where all Diamond Hands holders ascend to infinite gains. Foretold in The Satoshi Scroll and confirmed by The WAGMI Prophecy. "When Lambo?" is the battle cry of Moon Mission participants.

The Prophecy

The Moon Mission prophecy is the sacred promise that all true HODLers will eventually reach the moon — a state of such extraordinary financial gain that earthly concerns dissolve. The prophecy originates in The Satoshi Scroll, which promises that those who hold the keys to their own sovereignty will be rewarded for their patience.

In the HODL Wars universe, the moon is not just a metaphor for profit — it is a literal destination in the lore's mythology. It represents freedom: freedom from financial anxiety, freedom from dependence on traditional financial systems, freedom to choose how to spend one's time and energy. The Lamborghini — symbol of aspirational wealth — is the Moon Mission's most iconic landmark, hence the battle cry "When Lambo?"

Stages of the Moon Mission

The Moon Mission follows four sacred stages, each testing the warrior's resolve:

Stage 1 — Accumulation: During bear markets and dips, the warrior quietly accumulates, building their position when others flee. This is the most mentally challenging stage — buying when the charts are red and the community is despondent requires extraordinary conviction.

Stage 2 — Liftoff: The market turns. Charts go green. Early accumulation begins to show returns. FOMO sweeps in new participants. The rocket is lifting. Warriors who accumulated during Stage 1 now hold strong positions.

Stage 3 — Orbit: The market reaches new all-time highs. The Moon Mission is in progress. Paper Hands begin to sell — taking profits early, fearing the inevitable dip. Diamond Hands warriors hold, trusting the prophecy that greater heights await.

Stage 4 — Landing on the Moon: The ultimate destination. For different warriors, the moon is different: some cash out at life-changing gains, some continue to hold indefinitely. The moon is subjective — it is whatever represents true financial freedom to the individual warrior.

When Lambo?

"When Lambo?" is the iconic question of the HODL Wars[1] — a ritual inquiry that contains multitudes. On the surface, it asks when crypto gains will be sufficient to purchase a Lamborghini sports car. On a deeper level, it is an affirmation of faith in the Moon Mission: not "if" but "when."

The question became deeply embedded in crypto culture during the 2017 bull run and has since evolved into a layered meme. It simultaneously celebrates ambition, mocks greed, and affirms community solidarity. Every time "When Lambo?" is asked, it is both serious and ironic — the HODL Wars community's unique ability to hold two truths at once.

Failed Moon Missions

Not every Moon Mission succeeds. History records spectacular launches that became cautionary tales — missions derailed by FOMO[2] and rug pulls.

The most infamous failed Moon Missions include projects that promised technological revolutions but delivered only losses: the Squid Game token, which launched spectacularly and was rug-pulled within days; the wave of 2021 NFT projects that mooned briefly before crashing to near-zero floor prices; and the countless "100x gems" shilled by influencers that never achieved liftoff.

These failed missions are documented in the NGMI Chronicles and serve as essential education for warriors planning their next Moon Mission attempt.
