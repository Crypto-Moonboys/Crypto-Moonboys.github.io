Lore

Queen Sarah P-fly reigns supreme over the Sacred Chain, the digital backbone of Block Topia, her iron will shaping the Crypto Moonboys universe into a realm of both order and terror. As the sovereign ruler, she commands the GKniftyHEADS with a blend of charisma and cruelty, her decrees echoing through the metaverse as law. She is the architect and host of the Hard Fork Games, a brutal proving ground where factions vie for power, tokens, and survival under her watchful eye, often pitting rebels like Alfie 'The Bitcoin Kid' Blaze against her loyal enforcers.

Her influence permeates every corner of Block Topia, from the graffiti-tagged slums to the shimmering data hubs, making her both a protector of tradition and a target for rebellion, her rule a constant battle to maintain control in a world of chaos and defiance.

Real World Basis

Based on Sarah PU51FLY, real-world partner of Darren Cullen, whose creative influence shapes this iconic lore figure as a symbol of power and rebellion.
