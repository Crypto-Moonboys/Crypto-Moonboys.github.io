Forged from the agony of lost fortunes in their lore, the Furies emerged from the wreckage of betrayed trust, each member’s personal loss fueling their crusade for retribution, their pain transformed into a weapon that strikes at the heart of crypto’s darkest injustices.

Their NFT avatars are etched with symbols of betrayal—cracked coins and burned ledgers—each design a personal testament to the wrongs they’ve endured, serving as both a reminder of their mission and a warning to those who profit from the suffering of others.

A blood-coded oath binds them in legend, a smart contract rumored to be embedded in their core code, compelling the Furies to seek vengeance until every wrong is righted, a relentless drive that could one day turn even their closest allies into targets if justice demands sacrifice.
