⚔️ The RUGPULL MINERS

⚔️ Lore 📅 Last updated: March 2026 📂 Lore

The RUGPULL MINERS are the infamous deceivers of Block Topia, notorious profiteers who mine the blockchain’s darkest corners for opportunities to exploit through cunning scams. Masters of betrayal, they infiltrate factions with false promises, hoarding ill-gotten NFTs in hidden vaults, their schemes draining entire communities of digital wealth in mere cycles, leaving behind only shattered alliances and empty ledgers in the Crypto Moonboys universe. Their name is a curse, a warning of the greed that festers in the shadows of decentralized dreams.

The RUGPULL MINERS, deceptive profiteers in lore, have perfected the art of the blockchain scam, crafting elaborate schemes that drain entire factions of their digital wealth in mere cycles, leaving behind only shattered alliances and empty ledgers.

Their betrayal is legendary, often posing as allies to infiltrate trusting crews, only to execute devastating rug pulls that vanish their victims’ assets into hidden vaults, where they hoard ill-gotten NFTs as symbols of their twisted victories.

Cursed by the myth of striking corrupted code, their very essence is said to poison trust within Block Topia, with some believing that merely dealing with them risks tainting one’s own data with their malevolent glitches.

Their hidden vaults, scattered across the blockchain’s underbelly, are rumored to be guarded by rogue algorithms—sentinels of stolen wealth that attack any who dare to reclaim what the Miners have taken.

Despite their infamy, some desperate factions seek their expertise in dark code manipulation, willing to risk betrayal for the chance to wield the Miners’ forbidden knowledge against greater foes.

Guarded by their most elite operatives, this tool is the key to their most audacious scams, and its mere existence threatens the integrity of every transaction in the Crypto Moonboys universe.

Lore

In the shadowy depths of Block Topia, where trust is a currency more valuable than any NFT, The RUGPULL MINERS operate as the blockchain’s most despised yet cunning profiteers, their name a curse on the lips of every betrayed avatar. Their lore paints them as miners who, in their insatiable greed, struck cursed code instead of digital gold during the early days of the blockchain, a fateful error during the Great Hash Fracture that forever damned them to sow distrust and chaos wherever they tread.

Masters of deception, they weave intricate scams that drain resources from unsuspecting avatars and factions, often posing as allies with honeyed promises of shared wealth before pulling the rug out, vanishing with stolen assets into encrypted hideouts. Their operations are a blight on Block Topia, targeting everyone from the naive to the mighty, with even factions like The Nomad Bears falling prey to their schemes in moments of desperation.

The RUGPULL MINERS hoard their ill-gotten gains in hidden vaults, digital fortresses rumored to be guarded by corrupted AI sentinels programmed with the malice of their creators. Yet, their curse is a double-edged sword, for the cursed code they mine is said to taint their very essence, making them pariahs even among other rogues, forever marked by the blockchain as agents of ruin. Despite their infamy, their cunning ensures their survival, a persistent shadow that reminds all in Block Topia that trust is a luxury few can afford.
