🎭 The Princess

🎭 Lore 📅 Last updated: 2026-03-28 📂 Lore

Legends speak of the 'Cipher Palace,' a mythical server said to be The Princess’s hidden court, a digital fortress so heavily encrypted that even the most skilled hackers of Block Topia cannot locate its coordinates. Rumored to house ancient blockchain relics and forgotten protocols, it is said to be a place where time itself bends, allowing The Princess to foresee potential forks and shifts in power before they occur. Those few who claim to have glimpsed its shimmering gates describe a realm of pure data, where every decision made within its walls rewrites the metaverse’s future—an untouchable sanctuary that may hold the key to her enigmatic authority.
