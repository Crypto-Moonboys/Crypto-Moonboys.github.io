Lore

Snipey 'D-Man' Sirus cuts a solitary figure in the Crypto Moonboys universe, a deadly sharpshooter whose name is synonymous with precision and defense amidst the chaotic battlegrounds of Block Topia’s metaverse arenas. His reputation as an unyielding protector of key territories has made him a legend, his rifle’s scope a harbinger of doom for any faction daring to encroach on the zones he guards. Sirus’s origins are shrouded in mystery, with some tales claiming he was once a coder who lost everything in a catastrophic market crash, turning his grief into a weapon of unmatched accuracy, while others whisper he is a construct, an AI honed for combat during the earliest blockchain wars.

His sniper skills are most celebrated during events like the Hard Fork Games, where his shots have been known to dismantle entire strategies with a single pull of the trigger, earning him both reverence and dread. With allegiance unknown, Sirus operates as a lone wolf, his motives unclear—some say he defends for honor, others for a price yet to be named. In the neon-drenched chaos of Block Topia, to spot the glint of his scope is to know that judgment has already been passed, and survival is no longer guaranteed.

His presence is a wildcard in any conflict, a force that factions vie to sway, knowing that wherever Sirus aims, the balance of power shifts.
