HODL Warriors are a battle-hardened faction in the Crypto Moonboys universe, an elite brotherhood earned exclusively through the grueling trials of Hard Fork Games as per canon rule DB-3. As legends of Block Topia, they are both feared and revered, their presence on the battlefield a signal that surrender is not an option, no matter the cost.
