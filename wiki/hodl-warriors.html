Known Facts

HODL Warriors are bound by a sacred digital oath upon earning their rank in Hard Fork Games, a blockchain-etched vow that ties their avatars to the defense of their chosen allies in the HODL WARS. Breaking this oath risks exile to the Data Abyss, but upholding it grants access to exclusive ‘Warrior Forges,’ hidden server hubs where they can craft powerful NFT weapons imbued with the essence of past victories.

In the lore of Block Topia, HODL Warriors are rumored to possess a unique ability known as ‘Code Endurance,’ a glitch in their avatars’ programming born from surviving Hard Fork Games. This trait allows them to withstand server crashes or data corruption longer than other factions, making them invaluable in prolonged sieges against the blockchain overlords, though it comes at the cost of haunting visions of erased comrades.

Lore

HODL Warriors stand as the steel-forged elite of the Crypto Moonboys universe, a faction not born but brutally earned through the relentless trials of Hard Fork Games, as mandated by canon rule DB-3. These warriors embody unyielding resilience, forged in the digital inferno of Block Topia’s fiercest battlegrounds during the HODL WARS, where only the strongest avatars and players emerge with their rank intact. Each HODL Warrior is a unique entity, often tied to rare NFTs that bear the marks of their victories and scars of their defeats, making them icons of both fear and reverence among the 40 factions vying for control of the metropolis.

Their origins are steeped in the crypto mantra of ‘Hold On for Dear Life,’ a testament to their refusal to yield even when server hubs collapse or alliances betray them in the heat of battle. HODL Warriors are not just soldiers; they are symbols of perseverance, often leading assaults against Block Topia’s sentient walls or defending key data nodes from the blockchain overlords’ kill-bots. Their presence in MiDEViL HERO ARENA is legendary, where they wield their hard-earned power to claim tokens and relics, ensuring their faction’s dominance in the endless struggle for freedom and power within the digital cage of Block Topia.

Real World Basis

No direct real-world basis exists for HODL Warriors; they are a fictional faction inspired by the crypto term ‘HODL’ (Hold On for Dear Life), symbolizing perseverance and strength within the blockchain gaming and NFT community.
