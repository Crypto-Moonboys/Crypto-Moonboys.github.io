Known Facts

The HODL Warriors are bound by an ancient digital oath encoded into their avatars during the Hard Fork Games, a pact that compels them to serve the victorious faction with absolute loyalty until the next game cycle. This oath, visible as a glowing sigil on their armor, is both a badge of honor and a chain, ensuring their might is always tied to the whims of battlefield triumphs across the multi-chain ecosystem.

Each HODL Warrior carries a unique 'Battle Shard,' a fragment of code from their first victorious Hard Fork Game, which evolves with every battle, enhancing their combat algorithms. These shards are fiercely guarded, as they are rumored to contain echoes of past warriors’ strategies, making them invaluable relics that rival factions will kill to possess in the HODL WARS.

Lore

In the volatile expanse of the Crypto Moonboys universe, the HODL Warriors stand as an awe-inspiring army, an elite cadre of fighters sculpted from the raw chaos of the Hard Fork Games, as outlined in canon rule DB-3. These warriors are not merely born but meticulously earned, their ranks swelling with players and characters who have proven their worth in the unforgiving arenas of digital combat that span the multi-chain ecosystem of WAX, XRPL, SOL, and Bitcoin Cash.

As one of the 9 armies vying for supremacy in the HODL WARS saga, their allegiance is a coveted trophy, shifting with the tides of gameplay outcomes, aligning with whichever faction or leader can claim victory over their grueling challenges. Their presence on the battlefield is a storm of calculated ferocity, each warrior bearing the scars of virtual warfare, their avatars hardened by countless simulated deaths and rebirths.

The HODL Warriors are more than soldiers; they are legends whispered across Block Topia’s neon streets, a force that can break sieges or defend fortresses with equal prowess, embodying the ultimate prize for any faction daring to dream of ruling the digital frontier.
