The Great Dip

🌙 Community Lore 📅 Last updated: March 2026 📂 Lore
📋 Contents

The Great Dip is the recurring cataclysm of the HODL Wars — a sudden crash of the crypto markets that separates Diamond Hands from Paper Hands. Every Great Dip is preceded by the FOMO Plague and followed by the survivors marching toward the Moon Mission.

The Anatomy of a Dip

Every Great Dip follows the same mythological pattern — a six-phase cycle that plays out with eerie consistency across crypto history:

  1. Euphoria — Prices reach new highs, social media is flooded with moon talk, the FOMO Plague is at peak infectiousness. Even Paper Hands warriors are holding (for now).
  2. Peak — The market reaches its apex. In hindsight, this is the moment to sell. At the time, it feels like the beginning of a new era.
  3. Crash — A trigger event — regulatory news, exchange collapse, whale dump, macro event — sends prices tumbling. The charts turn red. Fear spreads.
  4. Capitulation — Paper Hands warriors sell in panic. Volume spikes. Prices cascade further. This is the darkest hour of the Great Dip, and paradoxically, often the best time to accumulate.
  5. Accumulation — Diamond Hands warriors and Whale Lords quietly buy. The selling pressure exhausts itself. Prices stabilise.
  6. Recovery — The market reverses. Those who held are vindicated. New warriors are born from the stories of those who survived.

Historical Great Dips

The chronicles record numerous Great Dips, each carving deep lessons into the collective memory of the HODL community:

The Crypto Winter of 2018[1] — Following Bitcoin's peak near $20,000 in December 2017, the market entered one of the most brutal bear cycles in crypto history. Bitcoin bottomed near $3,200 — a drawdown of approximately 84%. Ethereum fell from $1,400 to under $100. Most altcoins lost 95%+ of their value. This Great Dip forged the hardest Diamond Hands warriors in the lore.

The COVID Crash of March 2020 — In a single day (12 March 2020), Bitcoin fell roughly 50% from ~$8,000 to ~$4,000 amid global pandemic panic. This Great Dip was remarkable for its speed and for the extraordinary recovery that followed — Bitcoin would surpass $60,000 within 12 months, making COVID capitulators the most regretful Paper Hands figures of the era.

The Luna Collapse of 2022[2] — The implosion of the Terra/LUNA ecosystem in May 2022 wiped approximately $40 billion in value in days and precipitated a broader market crash that saw Bitcoin fall below $20,000. This Great Dip was unique in its lore significance because it was caused not by external forces but by internal betrayal — a rug pull of algorithmic scale, executed by market mechanics rather than malicious actors.

The Dip as Opportunity

"Buy the dip"[3] is the HODL Warriors' most celebrated tactical doctrine — the counter-intuitive response to the Great Dip that transforms catastrophe into opportunity. In the lore, every Great Dip is not a defeat but a gift: assets that were too expensive become accessible, and the departure of Paper Hands warriors strengthens the long-term position of those who remain.

The HODL Warriors who successfully accumulate during a Great Dip are celebrated as the wisest strategists in the chronicles. Their discipline in the face of universal panic — their ability to see the dip as opportunity rather than disaster — is the highest expression of Diamond Hands philosophy.
