Lore

Graffiti Kings stands as the bedrock faction of the Crypto Moonboys and GraffPUNKS universe, a legendary force born from the streets of London in 1983 and transformed into a digital juggernaut of rebellion on the blockchain. Founded by Darren Cullen (SER) and Trevor Fung, this collective has carried the unbuffable spirit of street art from the brick walls of Leake Street HQ to the virtual battlegrounds of Block Topia, where their influence shapes the very fabric of the Sacred Chain.

As the umbrella faction for GraffPUNKS and GKniftyHEADS, Graffiti Kings commands a sprawling network of 40 sub-factions, each carrying the torch of defiance through unique styles and strategies, whether clashing in HODL WARS or dominating the Hard Fork Games. Their real-world legacy of graffiti culture infuses the digital realm with authenticity, turning every NFT, every tag, and every battle into a statement against oppression, as they fight to keep the blockchain free from centralized control.

In the lore, Graffiti Kings are not just leaders but cultural icons, their murals in Block Topia serving as rallying points for rebels, their radio broadcasts on GraffPUNKS’ 24/7 station fueling the fires of resistance across the multi-chain ecosystem.

Real World Basis

Real-world street art collective based in London, founded by Darren Cullen (SER) and Trevor Fung, active since 1983, HQ at Leake Street.
