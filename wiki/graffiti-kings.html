Lore

Graffiti Kings is the raw, unquenchable origin of the Crypto Moonboys universe, a street-art collective born in the 1980s on London’s gritty streets under Darren Cullen’s visionary leadership, now the bedrock of the GKniftyHEADS digital rebellion. Their legacy of spray-painted defiance evolved from Croydon’s concrete walls into phygital murals and blockchain innovation, shaping Block Topia as a battleground for decentralized freedom. In lore, Graffiti Kings fuels every NFT drop and metaverse clash, a relentless spirit of street culture that inspires warriors to tag their resistance across virtual realms against any oppressive force.

Real World Basis

A real-world graffiti collective founded by Darren Cullen in the 1980s in Croydon, South London, which has transformed into a Web3 NFT powerhouse through GKniftyHEADS initiatives.
