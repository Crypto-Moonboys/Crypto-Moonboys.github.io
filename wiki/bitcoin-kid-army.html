Lore

The Bitcoin Kid Army is a defiant legion in the Crypto Moonboys universe, a band of escaped rebels who broke free from Block Topia’s suffocating walls under the leadership of Alfie 'The Bitcoin Kid' Blaze to wage war for digital liberty. Armed with graffiti-inspired NFTs and fueled by the untamed energy of street-art culture, they launch audacious assaults in metaverse battles, challenging centralized forces with every spray-painted strike. Their youthful rebellion serves as a rallying cry across Block Topia, inspiring outcasts to join their cause and forge a decentralized future amidst conflicts like the Triple Fork Event.

Real World Basis

A fictional army within GraffPUNKS lore, representing escape and resistance against centralized control in a digital narrative.
