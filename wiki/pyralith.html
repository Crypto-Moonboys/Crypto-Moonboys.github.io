Expeditions to locate this core have ended in disaster, with entire teams of warriors and coders consumed by sudden, inexplicable infernos before they could even glimpse their target. Some believe that destroying the Ember Core could banish PYRALITH forever, while others fear it might unleash her full potential, turning the metaverse into a permanent blaze.

PYRALITH’s destructive nature has birthed a grim tradition among Block Topia’s survivors, who mark the sites of her worst rampages with ‘Ash Altars,’ digital memorials constructed from the remnants of incinerated code as a warning to future warriors. These altars, often found in the aftermath of her appearances at events like the Triple Fork Event, serve as both tribute and cautionary tale, their flickering, glitch-ridden flames a pale echo of her true power. Factions use these sites to strategize against her, though many believe that no plan can withstand the unrelenting fury of PYRALITH’s fire.
