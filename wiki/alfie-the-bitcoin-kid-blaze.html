Lore

Alfie 'The Bitcoin Kid' Blaze is the blazing soul of rebellion in the Crypto Moonboys universe, a fearless leader who shattered the confines of Block Topia’s walls to lead the escaped Bitcoin Kids in a relentless fight against centralized tyranny. Wielding a spray can and blockchain savvy, he guides the Bitcoin Kid Army through the digital wilderness, striking rival factions with guerrilla tactics rooted in street-art defiance during metaverse battles.

His growing legend inspires digital outcasts to rally under his banner, forging a path to a decentralized future amidst conflicts like the Triple Fork Event, often clashing with forces such as the Street Kingdoms. His influence extends to pivotal moments where his strategic mind turns the tide, making him a beacon of hope for those yearning for freedom.

Real World Basis

Inspired by the ethos of rebellion and innovation within the crypto and street art communities, though not directly tied to a specific individual.
