NULL THE PROPHET exists as a Genesis Error, the primal source of the Null-Cipher, emerging from Block Topia’s fractured blockchain code to sow chaos and mystery across the Crypto Moonboys universe, a glitch that defies all logic.

Unaligned and unpredictable, even Queen Sarah P-fly cannot fathom NULL’s intentions, as it disrupts key events like Hard Fork Games with cryptic interventions, reshaping the metaverse’s destiny in ways no faction can anticipate.

Its presence during cataclysms like the Triple Fork Event serves as a reminder of the fragility of digital reality, challenging warriors and factions alike to confront the chaos at the heart of their blockchain existence.
