The FOMO Plague

🌙 Community Lore 📅 Last updated: March 2026 📂 Lore
📋 Contents

The FOMO Plague (Fear Of Missing Out) is a recurring epidemic in the HODL Wars universe, spread via social media, influencer shilling, and parabolic price charts. It converts rational traders into Paper Hands capitulators and fuels Rug Pull Wars. Documented extensively in NGMI Chronicles.

Symptoms of FOMO

The FOMO Plague is identifiable by a distinct symptom cluster that every experienced HODL Warrior learns to recognise in themselves and others[1]:

  • Panic buying at all-time highs — The FOMO-infected warrior buys when charts are most extended, driven by fear that they will miss the next leg up.
  • Abandoning DYOR — Research is replaced by urgency. "There's no time to check the whitepaper; the chart is going parabolic NOW."
  • Following influencers blindly — Influencer calls that previously would have been verified are now acted upon immediately, without independent verification.
  • Emotional decision-making — Logical frameworks are overridden by emotional urgency. The infected warrior feels they must act immediately or forever miss their chance.
  • Overexposure — FOMO-infected warriors risk more capital than their strategy calls for, drawn in by the prospect of accelerated gains.

The Great FOMO Events

The chronicles record several Great FOMO Events — market moments where the Plague reached pandemic scale:

Dogecoin 2021[2] — A viral tweet from Elon Musk combined with Reddit coordination drove Dogecoin to all-time highs, infecting millions of new retail participants with FOMO at precisely the wrong moment. Countless newcomers entered crypto for the first time during this FOMO outbreak, many at or near the peak, becoming instant NGMI Chronicles entries.

The NFT Boom 2021 — FOMO at an almost incomprehensible scale. Six-figure JPEGs, celebrity endorsements, and viral stories of overnight millionaires drove mass participation in the NFT market at valuations that proved unsustainable. The subsequent collapse left millions of warriors holding worthless PFP collections.

The Luna/UST Narrative (2022) — Terra's promises of 20% yields on UST stablecoin deposits triggered massive FOMO-driven inflows, attracting billions of dollars before the ecosystem's fatal flaw — its algorithmic design — triggered the collapse that wiped out an estimated $40 billion in two weeks.

Inoculation: DYOR

The only proven inoculation against the FOMO Plague is a rigorous commitment to DYOR — Do Your Own Research. Diamond Hands warriors who have studied The Satoshi Scroll and built their investment framework through careful research are largely immune to FOMO outbreaks, because their conviction is based on fundamentals rather than momentum.

The DYOR inoculation works by creating a mental firewall: before acting on any market signal — whether from social media, a price chart, or an influencer call — the warrior pauses to verify the underlying fundamentals. This pause is often sufficient to break the FOMO infection cycle.
