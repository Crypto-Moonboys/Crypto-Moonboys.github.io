🎭 The MOONLORDS

🎭 Lore 📅 Last updated: 2026-03-28 📂 Lore

The MOONLORDS, aspirational rulers in lore, are driven by a singular vision of ultimate dominance, believing it their birthright to control every digital domain in Block Topia, a goal they pursue with ruthless precision and unshakeable conviction.

As descendants of the first crypto pioneers in myth, they claim a divine mandate to rule, their propaganda woven into NFT drops and avatars that subtly indoctrinate others into accepting their vision of a unified, MOONLORD-led blockchain.

Their rumored orbital bases, hidden in encrypted blockchain clouds, are said to house vast arsenals of digital weaponry—code capable of rewriting entire sectors of Block Topia to crush resistance before it can even form.

Clashes with independents like The Nomad Bears are legendary, often fought in the untamed wilds where the MOONLORDS deploy overwhelming force to stamp out defiance, viewing every unclaimed territory as a future province of their empire.

Their ambition is matched by their cunning, as they manipulate lesser factions into serving their cause, promising protection or wealth only to absorb their resources and autonomy into the ever-expanding MOONLORD domain.
