Solana (SOL)

✅ Verified📅 Last updated: 2024-01📂 Cryptocurrencies
📋 Contents

Solana (SOL) is a high-performance public blockchain renowned for its extremely fast transaction speeds and low fees. Launched in 2020 by Anatoly Yakovenko and the Solana Foundation, it has become a leading platform for DeFi, NFTs, and consumer-facing Web3 applications.

Overview

Solana achieves throughput of up to 65,000 transactions per second (TPS) on its base layer — orders of magnitude faster than Ethereum or Bitcoin. This is made possible by its unique Proof of History (PoH) consensus mechanism combined with Proof of Stake.

Proof of History

PoH is a cryptographic clock that creates a historical record proving that an event occurred at a specific moment in time. This allows the network to reach consensus without all validators needing to communicate about time ordering — dramatically increasing throughput.

Ecosystem

Solana hosts a vibrant ecosystem including Jupiter (leading DEX aggregator), Magic Eden (top NFT marketplace), Phantom (popular wallet), and Helium (decentralised wireless). Meme coins like Bonk and WIF also launched to massive popularity on Solana.

Solana experienced several high-profile outages in 2021–2022, raising reliability questions, but has since improved significantly in stability.

Community Views

Solana has an extremely vocal and passionate community. Fans frequently debate "Solana vs Ethereum" on Twitter, Reddit, and Discord. The community is known for coining terms like "SOL szn" and defending Solana's performance metrics against critics.
