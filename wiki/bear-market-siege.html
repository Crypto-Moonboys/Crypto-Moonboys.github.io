The Bear Market Siege

🌙 Community Lore 📅 Last updated: March 2026 📂 Lore
📋 Contents

The Bear Market Siege is the prolonged dark chapter of the HODL Wars when the markets enter a sustained downtrend. Diamond Hands warriors hunker down. Paper Hands flee. Whale Lords accumulate. The Siege ends only when the Moon Mission resumes.

The Psychology of the Siege

Where The Great Dip is a sudden shock, the Bear Market Siege is psychological warfare — a grinding, relentless attrition of hope and conviction designed to exhaust even the most committed Diamond Hands warriors. The Siege operates through despair, FUD (Fear, Uncertainty, Doubt), and the constant temptation to sell.

The stages of Siege psychology follow a well-documented arc:

  • Denial: "This is just a dip. The moon mission resumes soon."
  • Anger: "Whales are manipulating the market. The system is rigged."
  • Bargaining: "If it just recovers to my buy price, I'll sell and walk away."
  • Despair: "Crypto is dead. This time it's different. Maybe I should just sell."
  • Acceptance: "This is the accumulation phase. I am buying the siege."

Only warriors who reach Acceptance survive the Siege with their Diamond Hands intact. Those who capitulate at Despair become casualties documented in the NGMI Chronicles.

Great Bear Sieges in History

The chronicles record two Great Bear Sieges of the modern era:

The First Siege — Crypto Winter 2018–2019[1] — Following the 2017 ICO boom and Bitcoin's peak near $20,000, the market entered a devastating bear cycle that lasted approximately 12 months. Bitcoin lost 84% of its value; most altcoins lost 95%+. Projects with no fundamental value were wiped out entirely. This Siege forged the most battle-hardened generation of HODL Warriors.

The Second Siege — Crypto Winter 2022–2023[2] — Triggered by the collapse of the Terra/LUNA ecosystem in May 2022, the failure of crypto exchange FTX in November 2022, and a broader macro environment of rising interest rates, this Siege saw Bitcoin fall from $69,000 to below $16,000. Unlike the First Siege, this one was characterised not just by market losses but by institutional failures and spectacular high-profile collapses — a Siege of betrayal as much as market forces.

Survival Guide

HODL Warriors who survived the Great Sieges share a consistent playbook for enduring the Bear Market Siege:

Dollar-Cost Average (DCA): Continue accumulating at regular intervals regardless of price. Remove emotion from the equation. The Siege price is a gift; future you will be grateful for every sats stacked during the dark.

Cold Storage: Move assets off exchanges and into hardware wallets. The Siege is when exchanges fail; warriors who hold their own keys cannot be rugged by institutional collapse.

DYOR: Use the Siege's quieter market to do the research you didn't have time for during the bull run. Study the fundamentals. Identify which projects deserve to survive and concentrate conviction accordingly.

Maintain the Community: The Siege is survived together. The HODL Wars community is the warrior's greatest resource — shared conviction, shared intelligence, and shared reminder that every Siege in history has ended with a new Moon Mission.
