Lore

The Croydon Tower Blocks are the gritty cradle of defiance in the Crypto Moonboys universe, a real-world South London location where Graffiti Kings first sprayed their mark in the 1980s, igniting the street-art movement that evolved into GKniftyHEADS. In lore, these towering concrete giants are sacred ground, a symbolic origin point where the spirit of decentralized rebellion was born, inspiring every NFT mural and metaverse battle across Block Topia. They remain a pilgrimage site for digital warriors, a stark reminder of the raw, unfiltered energy that fuels their fight for freedom against centralized oppression.

Real World Basis

A real-world location in Croydon, South London, UK, central to the early history of Graffiti Kings since the 1980s, marking the start of their cultural impact.
