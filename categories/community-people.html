👥 The Humans Behind the Movement

Crypto Moonboys is not a faceless project — it's a community of real artists, writers, builders, and believers who have been grinding in the blockchain space long before it was fashionable. At its core are the people who built London's Graffiti Kings into a cultural institution, and then had the vision to take that legacy on-chain. 🌍

🔑 Key Figures

  • 🎨 Darren Cullen (SER) — founder of Graffiti Kings, artist name SER, the driving creative force behind the entire universe. A pioneering figure of UK graffiti culture since the early 1980s, he pivoted the Graffiti Kings into Web3 during the COVID-19 lockdown — turning four decades of street art into a blockchain legacy. Commissioned by major brands, official artist for the 2012 Olympics, and now painting the blockchain itself.
  • 🔗 boneidolink — community contributor and builder within the Crypto Moonboys universe, active across Web3 channels and NFT ecosystems
  • ✍️ jilliangodsil — writer, journalist, and blockchain advocate who has contributed to the Crypto Moonboys narrative and broader Web3 community discourse
  • 📣 iangharrison — community voice and contributor helping amplify the GraffPUNKS message across digital channels
  • 🎙️ iamcharliebuster — community figure active in the Crypto Moonboys social ecosystem, helping build the grassroots following

🌍 Community Channels & Events

The Crypto Moonboys community is robustly multi-platform — active across Telegram, Facebook, and live events both physical and virtual. Key milestones include:

  • 🏛️ Graffiti Queens NFT Show — the largest online art show in Decentraland, spotlighting female graffiti artists and proving the community's commitment to inclusivity within the traditionally male-dominated street art world
  • 🎓 Youth workshops — government-endorsed, community-run workshops teaching street art as a tool for creative expression and social change — connecting the next generation to the GK legacy
  • 🎉 Digital art exhibitions — regular events in the metaverse celebrating the full GKniftyHEADS universe and its factions
  • 📻 GraffPUNKS Network Radio — the 24/7 live community soundtrack keeping everyone connected at graffpunks.live
