Anti-establishment, DIY, community-powered. Punk isn't dead — it's on-chain.

Punk Is On-Chain — The GraffPUNKS ethos runs through everything Crypto Moonboys stands for. DIY, no gatekeepers, community over corporations. The Squeaky Pinks and BanksyPunks carry this torch into the Web3 era.

⚡ The GraffPUNKS Network

The GraffPUNKS are more than a faction — they're a decentralised cultural movement and digital extension of street art into blockchain communities. Operating out of graffpunks.live, the GraffPUNKS Network is a blockchain-powered radio station, publishing platform, and community hub running 24/7 across multiple chains. 🌐

At the heart of the network is the $PUNK Coin — a reward token earned by listeners and participants. Tune in, earn crypto, support the culture. No algorithms. No playlist curators. No corporate sponsors. Just the community picking the music and building the vibe. 🎵💰

🎸 Factions & Collectives

Punk Culture in the Crypto Moonboys universe manifests through several distinct factions, each with their own identity and energy:

  • ⚡ The GraffPUNKS — the founding punk collective, bridging street art rebellion with blockchain decentralisation. Anti-establishment, DIY, on-chain.
  • 🎸 The Squeaky Pinks — punk band and cultural collective within the Crypto Moonboys universe, embodying the raw, unpolished energy of classic UK punk
  • ⚡ The EVM Punks — EVM-native punk culture, treating the Ethereum Virtual Machine as the new public wall to be tagged and claimed
  • 🏴 BanksyPunks — referencing the anonymous street art tradition that proves you can shake the mainstream from the outside, with no name and no permission

🚫 The Anti-Corporate Ethos

What makes the GraffPUNKS authentically punk in the Web3 era? They reject the "dead-end Discord culture" of mainstream NFT projects — the empty hype, the roadmap theatre, the influencer shilling. Instead:

  • 🛠️ Build first — real radio, real publications, real NFTs with utility before promises
  • 🤝 Community over clout — $PUNK Coin rewards actual engagement, not just big wallet holders
  • 🔒 No gatekeepers — anyone with a WAX wallet can join, collect, listen, and earn
  • 🎨 Art-first — four decades of real street art culture backing every on-chain move
