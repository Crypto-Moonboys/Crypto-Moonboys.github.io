💡 Core Concepts in the Moonboys Ecosystem

The Crypto Moonboys didn't just adopt blockchain technology — they lived it before they coded it. The concepts documented here aren't textbook definitions: they're frameworks that directly inform how the GraffPUNKS network, GKniftyHEADS NFTs, and HODL Wars mythology operate. 🌐

🏦 DeFi as Financial Rebellion

Decentralised Finance resonates deeply with the Crypto Moonboys ethos. Graffiti culture was born in communities historically excluded from traditional finance — the same communities that DeFi is designed to serve. Remove the banks. Remove the middlemen. Let artists get paid directly, transparently, immediately, through smart contracts that can't be manipulated by institutions. 💸

The GKniftyHEADS royalty mechanism is DeFi in action: every NFT secondary sale automatically triggers a payment to the original artist via smart contract. No label taking a cut. No gallery commission. Just code and community. ⛓️

🎨 NFTs as Artist Empowerment

Non-Fungible Tokens solved one of graffiti art's oldest problems: how do you prove you made it, and how do you get paid when it's resold? For four decades, street artists created value that galleries and brands captured. NFTs change the equation — on-chain provenance means the original artist is always credited, and smart contract royalties mean they're always paid. 🖼️

🌐 Web3 as the Next Frontier

Web3 — the decentralised, user-owned internet — is the natural home for the Crypto Moonboys universe. No platform can deplatform the community. No algorithm can bury the culture. No company can shut down the radio station. The GraffPUNKS Network exists on-chain, which means it exists forever. That's not hyperbole — that's immutability. 🔒
