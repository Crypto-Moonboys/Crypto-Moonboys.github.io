Activation flow & live commands

Your account link happens entirely through the Telegram bot. This page explains the flow and lists every live /gk* command. When you click the one-time link from /gklink, you are redirected to community.html?gklink=… which confirms your identity server-side and marks you as competition-active in your browser.

1

Join & register

Join the Telegram group, then run /gkstart in the bot. This registers your profile, awards first-launch XP, and shows you what to do next.

2

Generate your link

Run /gklink in the bot. The bot sends you a private, one-time link that expires in 15 minutes. Each call invalidates any previous unused tokens.

3

Click the link

Tap the link in Telegram. It opens community.html?gklink=… in your browser, which calls the API to validate the token and confirm your identity.

4

Competition active

The page confirms success and marks your browser as telegram_linked. Leaderboard rankings, XP, votes, faction alignment, and seasonal scoring are now unlocked.

Account sync does not happen on this page. As explained above, the actual identity confirmation happens at community.html?gklink=<token> after you run /gklink in the bot.

Live /gk* commands

Command What it does
/gkstartRegister your profile and earn first-launch XP. Run this first.
/gklinkGenerate a one-time 15-minute link to connect your Telegram identity to the website. This is the account sync command.
/gkstatusView your current XP total, level, and faction alignment.
/gkseasonSee the current season window, end date, and scoring rules.
/gkleaderboardShow the community XP leaderboard.
/gkquestsList active lore quests and missions.
/gkfaction [name]View your faction or join one by passing its name as an argument.
/gkunlinkInvalidate all your current link tokens (use if a token is compromised).
/gkhelpPrint the full command list in the bot.
/dailyClaim your daily XP reward (once per 24 hours).
/solveSubmit an answer to an active lore quest.

Legacy aliases /start, /help, /link, /leaderboard, /quest, and /faction are still supported but the /gk* prefix versions are preferred.
