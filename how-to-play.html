Moonboys How-To-Play & Systems Manual

This is the player guide for the Moonboys ecosystem. Login with Telegram, earn XP through arcade games, and enter Block Topia when you have enough XP.

🕹️ Arcade Score Layer 🏆 Leaderboard Rank Layer 🏙️ Block Topia 🔗 Telegram Identity Layer ⚡ XP Economy

A) What this website is

The Crypto Moonboys site is one connected ecosystem made of four public layers:

🕹️ Arcade

Free-to-play skill games where you generate score and compete.

📚 Wiki

The lore, factions, characters, events, and strategic context of the world.

⚔️ Battle Chamber

Community intelligence layer: missions, activity, and social coordination.

🏙️ Block Topia

Clean 2-player isometric map base. Requires Telegram-linked account and XXX XP to enter. More gameplay coming.

They are connected, but not identical: score determines ranking, XP powers survival/progression, gems fuel upgrades, and your synced identity determines whether progress is persistent or browser-local.

B) Arcade explained

  • Arcade games are free to play.
  • Your run creates a score for the leaderboard.
  • Score is not XP. Leaderboard rank uses score, not your XP stash.
  • Accepted scores can convert into Block Topia XP when your Telegram identity is synced.
  • If you are unsynced, you can still play and post local run data, but long-term progression stays local/browser-only.

C) Telegram sync (required for stored progression)

Telegram sync is your identity handshake with the live system. Without it, the server cannot safely attach progression to a persistent player identity.

  1. Open the official bot: @WIKICOMSBOT.
  2. Run /gkstart (initial bot activation).
  3. Run /gklink.
  4. Open the link returned by the bot and complete link confirmation.
  5. Return to Arcade/Leaderboard/Block Topia and confirm sync is green/linked.
  • When linked: accepted score-to-XP conversion and progression can persist server-side.
  • If sync expires: your identity state can go red/invalid until you relink with /gklink.
  • If not linked: you can play, but progression is local-only and does not behave like full persistent progression.

F) How to earn XP (practical guide)

Improve score quality

  • Master 2–3 games instead of spreading effort thinly.
  • Prioritise clean runs over risky early-score spikes.
  • Use leaderboard refresh cycles to target realistic rank jumps.

Convert score into XP

  • Stay Telegram-linked so accepted runs can persist and convert.
  • If sync turns red, relink with /gklink before long sessions.
  • Aim for consistent runs — accepted runs beat one-off spikes.

G) FAQ / quick troubleshooting

I’m linked but can’t enter Block Topia

Most common cause: your current XP is below the entry threshold (XXX XP required). Check your XP total and keep playing to earn more.

I played a game but got no XP

Leaderboard score and XP are separate. XP comes from accepted score conversions when your Telegram identity is synced.

Why does it say local only?

Your session is currently unsynced. Runs still work, but progression is stored in your browser session, not a server-backed profile.

What does “accepted score” mean?

An accepted score is a run the system validates for ranking and XP conversion. Only accepted runs are eligible for XP conversion.

Why is sync red?

Your Telegram link likely expired or failed validation. Relink via /gklink and re-open the link from the bot.

What if auth expired?

Run /gklink again, complete the new link handshake, then refresh your game page before continuing.
