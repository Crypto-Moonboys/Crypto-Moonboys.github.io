How to Play Crypto Moonboys

The complete step-by-step guide to entering and progressing through the living Web3 wiki system.

The Official Route

Read → Play → Earn XP → Link → Battle Chamber → Block Topia → Build

This is the core journey that every player follows. Each step builds on the last, creating a complete progression system from curious visitor to active builder.

Step 1: Read the Living Wiki

Start by exploring the wiki. The pages are not just static information — they are the living memory of the Crypto Moonboys universe. Understanding the lore, factions, history, mechanics, and culture makes every action more meaningful and powerful.

You don’t need to read everything before playing, but the deeper your knowledge of the world, the more rewarding your experience becomes.

Step 2: Play the Arcade

The arcade is the first real action layer of the system. Every game you play generates a score. Scores can appear on leaderboards and contribute to Arcade XP when accepted and synced through Telegram.

Current active games include Invaders 3008, Asteroid Fork, Breakout Bullrun, Crystal Quest, Pac-Chain, Snake Run, Tetris Block Topia, and Block Topia Quest Maze.

Important: Score is not the same as Arcade XP. Score measures your performance in a single run. Arcade XP measures consistent participation over time.

Step 3: Earn Arcade XP

Arcade XP is the true progression currency of Crypto Moonboys. It is earned when you play supported games, link your Telegram account, and have your runs accepted by the system.

“XP is not given. It is earned through activity.”

Currently, 50 Arcade XP is required to unlock Block Topia Live City.

Step 4: Link Your Telegram Identity

This is the most critical step for long-term progress. Without linking Telegram, your progress is temporary and browser-only. Once linked, your Arcade XP, faction status, and future progression become persistent and server-backed.

Use the Incubator page and run /gklink in @WIKICOMSBOT to connect your identity permanently.

Step 5: Enter the Battle Chamber

The Battle Chamber is the public proof layer. This is where your activity becomes visible to the entire community. XP movement, faction alignment, missions, and community status all appear here.

If you take action, it shows. If you stay passive, nothing appears.

Step 6: Enter Block Topia Live City

Once you reach 50 Arcade XP and have a linked Telegram account, you can enter Block Topia Live City — the current gated 2-player Colyseus multiplayer survival and mission prototype.

This is a focused, high-stakes multiplayer experience featuring NPCs, combat, phases, objectives, extraction, and upgrades.

Step 7: Build

The final stage is contribution. Whether that means creating content, joining factions, shaping lore, or eventually helping build City Block Topia / Neon Sprawl — the system rewards those who move from player to builder.
