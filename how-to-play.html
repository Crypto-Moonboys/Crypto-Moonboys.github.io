How to Play Crypto Moonboys

Crypto Moonboys is a living Web3 wiki system. You read the lore, play the arcade, earn Arcade XP, link your Telegram identity, appear in the Battle Chamber, unlock Block Topia, and move deeper into the world.

Read → Play → Earn XP → Link → Battle Chamber → Block Topia → Build

1. Quick Start

Start here if you are new.

Step 1 — Read
Explore the wiki. The wiki is the memory layer. It explains the lore, characters, factions, HODL Wars, GraffPUNKS, GKniftyHEADS, Bitcoin Kid, Block Topia, and the wider Moonboys universe.

Step 2 — Play
Enter the arcade. The games are the first action layer. You do not need to understand every piece of lore before playing. The arcade gives you something immediate to do.

Step 3 — Earn Arcade XP
Play accepted runs. Your score is your performance in a game. Arcade XP is progression earned through accepted activity when your identity is linked correctly. Score and XP are not the same thing.

Step 4 — Link Telegram
Telegram sync turns you from a temporary browser visitor into a persistent identity. Without Telegram, the system cannot fully remember you.

Step 5 — Watch the Battle Chamber
The Battle Chamber is the public proof layer. It shows activity, movement, leaderboard pressure, faction signals, and who is showing up.

Step 6 — Unlock Block Topia
Block Topia is currently a clean 2-player isometric map base. Entry requires Telegram linked + 50 Arcade XP. Deeper Block Topia systems are future layers.

Step 7 — Build
The long-term route is not just playing. It is building, creating, owning, expanding, and helping shape the culture engine.

2. What Crypto Moonboys Is

Crypto Moonboys is not just a game, not just a wiki, not just NFTs, and not just another Web3 community. It is a living Web3 wiki system. The wiki explains the universe. The arcade gives you action. XP records proof of action. Telegram gives identity. The Battle Chamber shows proof. Factions create belonging. Block Topia opens the next layer.

4. Score vs Arcade XP

Score is how well you performed in one run.
Arcade XP is progression earned from accepted activity.
A high score can place you on a leaderboard.
Arcade XP proves repeated movement through the system.
XP is not a financial reward.
XP does not mean passive income.
XP is proof that you showed up.

5. Telegram Sync

Telegram sync connects your browser activity to a persistent identity. Use the Telegram bot and the site sync flow so your Arcade XP can be remembered. Telegram is not just a chat link. It is the identity bridge for the living wiki.

  • Link Telegram
  • Play accepted arcade runs
  • Check your XP status
  • Return to Battle Chamber

6. Arcade Games

The arcade is not a side feature. It is the activation layer. Without the arcade, the wiki is knowledge. With the arcade, the wiki becomes playable.

Game types:

  • Shooter games: reaction, survival, boss pressure
  • Maze games: routing, risk control, timing
  • Snake games: survival, combos, pickups
  • Breakout games: physics, pressure, combos
  • Puzzle/block games: planning, endurance, clean scoring

Different games may support different faction effects and cross-game modifiers.

7. Daily Missions

Daily Missions give players a reason to return and a reason to try different games. Missions may ask players to survive longer, complete runs, collect rare rewards, hit score targets, or support faction progress.

If a mission system is local or partial, do not claim it is a full server-backed reward system unless confirmed live.

8. Factions

Factions create belonging. A user who joins a site is weakly attached. A user who joins a faction has a side.

Why join a faction?
Faction choice affects supported arcade games through in-run perks, upgrade bias, and faction signal. Faction effects apply where supported — not every perk applies to every game.

Current factions:

Diamond Hands:
Best for long survival and clean play.
Perks: reduced chaos pressure, survival score bonus after time threshold, late-game scaling. Best for players who survive long and play clean.

HODL Warriors:
Best for defense and safer runs.
Perks: extra starting shield where supported, reduced chaos penalty, defensive upgrade options appear more often. Best for players who want stability and survivability.

GraffPUNKS:
Best for chaos, combos, and high-risk score bursts.
Perks: higher chaos event frequency, better combo potential, higher rare spawn chance, more aggressive score opportunities. Best for players who want fast, volatile, high-risk runs.

Factions are crew identity. They connect playstyle, lore, and future competition. Faction signal updates after accepted activity.

9. Battle Chamber

The Battle Chamber is the proof wall. It shows movement. It makes action visible. It is where the system stops being static pages and starts feeling alive.

10. Block Topia

Block Topia is the next gated layer. Live now, it is a clean 2-player isometric map base. Access requires Telegram linked + 50 Arcade XP. That gate proves the user moved through the route: read, play, earn, link, enter.

NPCs, battles, faction zones, deeper economy, and seasonal HODL Wars are future layers unless already shipped.
