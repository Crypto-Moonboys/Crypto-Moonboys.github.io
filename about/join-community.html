⚔️ Become a HODL Warrior

The first step in joining the Crypto Moonboys community is becoming a HODL Warrior — choosing to hold your conviction through every test the market throws at you:

💎 Step 1: Reject Paper Hands

  • ⚡️ Hold through the dips — when the market drops 20%, 30%, 50%, the paper hands fold. The HODL Warriors hold. Every dip is a buying opportunity, not a reason to retreat.
  • 🐻 Hold through the crashes — bear markets are not the end of the story. They are the training ground. The HODL Warriors who survived 2018, 2020, and 2022 were handsomely rewarded.
  • 😤 Hold through the FUD — when mainstream media declares crypto dead (for the 400th time), when governments threaten regulation, when exchange collapses cause panic — the HODL Warriors hold. FUD is fuel for the diamond hands.
  • ⏰ Hold for the long term — the HODL Warrior's time horizon is measured in years and decades, not days and weeks. Patient conviction beats anxious trading every time.

Step 2: Stack Sats, DCA, Build Your Position

  • 💰 Start stacking sats — even one dollar a day in Bitcoin. Open a wallet, set up a DCA purchase on your preferred exchange, and start accumulating. The House of #RackinSATS starts with a single sat.
  • 📅 DCA (Dollar Cost Average) — invest a fixed amount at regular intervals regardless of price. Remove emotion from the equation. Let time and math do the work.
  • 🔒 Self-custody — "not your keys, not your coins." Learn to manage your own wallet. The true HODL Warrior holds their own keys.
  • 📚 Learn as you stack — every sat you stack should come with knowledge. Read the wiki. Understand what you own and why you own it.

  • 📖 Read the HODL Wars Universe — all 13 lore articles tell the complete story of the Crypto Moonboys mythology. Understand the Diamond Hands Army, the Paper Hands Legion, the Bear Market Siege, the WAGMI Prophecy.

  • 🌙 Live the WAGMI Prophecy — internalise the belief that if you hold, if you build, if you remain convicted — you will make it. All of you.
