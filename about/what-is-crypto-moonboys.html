What is Crypto Moonboys?

The living Web3 wiki where 40 years of street rebellion becomes playable. The pages explain the world. The arcade lets you enter it.

The Core Truth

Crypto Moonboys is a living Web3 wiki.

Not a static encyclopedia. Not just an arcade. Not just an NFT collection. It is a reactive system — a world with memory, proof, movement, and a clear path for anyone to go from reader to player to builder.

The shortest possible definition: “The pages explain the world. The arcade lets you enter it. The more you show up, the more the system opens.”

Where It Comes From

Crypto Moonboys is the digital evolution of Graffiti Kings (GK) — a 40+ year London graffiti movement that began in the early 1980s. What started as tagging trains and running pirate radio evolved into a philosophy of Legacy Over Hype.

When blockchain arrived, GK recognized something profound: graffiti was the original blockchain — a decentralized, permissionless public ledger of truth.

The Living Wiki Philosophy

Most wikis are dead archives. Crypto Moonboys is designed to be alive.

  • Pages explain the universe
  • Arcade games let you physically enter that universe
  • XP proves you showed up
  • Telegram makes your identity persistent
  • Battle Chamber makes your actions visible
  • Block Topia Live City is the current gated multiplayer layer
  • City Block Topia / Neon Sprawl is the deeper playable-wiki territory coming next

The Three Block Topia Layers

Block Topia Live City — Current gated 2-player Colyseus multiplayer survival/mission prototype (live now).

Block Topia Quest Maze — Separate arcade/RPG score game and Arcade XP path (live now).

City Block Topia / Neon Sprawl — Deeper living-wiki territory game (planned integration).
