🌙 What Is Crypto Moonboys?

Crypto Moonboys is the public front door into the Graffiti Kings / GKniftyHEADS Web3 culture system. It connects street art, games, NFTs, XP, factions, lore, live radio, Telegram identity and creator-owned IP into one operating layer.

This is not just a wiki and not just an NFT collection. It is the floor: the place where people walk in, play, earn XP, enter deeper systems, collect or burn assets, join factions, build with IP and help shape where the universe goes next.

👑 Who Built It

Crypto Moonboys comes from Graffiti Kings / GKniftyHEADS: a street-born culture brand with decades of graffiti, street art, pirate radio, music, guerrilla marketing, workshops, commercial campaigns and community activation behind it.

The Web3 pivot does not replace that history. It extends it. Physical walls became digital walls. Tags became wallets. Characters became playable identities. Collectors became potential builders.

The core flow is simple:

The Crypto Moonboys universe is multi-chain and multi-layered. WAX is the main territory for current NFT and game activity, with wider expansion across XRPL, Solana and Bitcoin Cash. The world includes Block Topia, the Street Kingdoms, the 40 GK factions, HODL Wars, the Bitcoin Kid Army and the wider 3008 lore.

🎯 Mission

GK does not exist to create another passive claim loop. The mission is to turn attention into action, action into ownership, and ownership into creation.

The old internet trained people to scroll, like, claim and leave. Crypto Moonboys is built around the opposite behaviour: show up, act, compete, build, own, and bring others with you.
