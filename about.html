🌙 What Is Crypto Moonboys?

Crypto Moonboys is a fan-driven, multi-chain crypto encyclopedia and lore universe — the wiki for the GKniftyHEADS community. It combines real crypto education, legendary HODL Wars mythology, NFT culture, and graffiti street art aesthetics into one interconnected, ever-expanding knowledge base.

Born from the collision of street art culture and crypto philosophy, Crypto Moonboys is where graffiti meets blockchain, where lore meets education, and where the community defines the canon. No gatekeepers. No sign-ups. Just knowledge, culture, and WAGMI energy.

📋 Citation Policy

All wiki articles cite their sources transparently. We accept community sources (social media, YouTube, podcasts), on-chain data, official project documentation, and reputable crypto publications. Every article includes a references section.

Source types used across the wiki:
