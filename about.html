About Crypto Moonboys

Crypto Moonboys is a living Web3 wiki system built from street culture, lore, arcade action, identity, XP, factions, and future builder layers. It is not another project asking people to wait. It gives people a world to read, a system to enter, and actions that leave a mark.

1. The Short Version

Read the lore. Play the arcade. Earn Arcade XP. Link Telegram. Enter the Battle Chamber. Unlock Block Topia. Build deeper into the world.

2. Not Just A Wiki

A normal wiki stores information. Crypto Moonboys uses the wiki as the memory layer of a playable universe. The pages explain the world. The arcade lets you act inside it.

3. Not Just A Game

The arcade is important, but it is not the whole project. The games are the entry layer. They create movement, XP, faction contribution, and reasons to return.

4. Not Just NFTs

NFTs are not treated as empty collectibles or profit promises. In this world, NFTs are artefacts, identity markers, ownership signals, and possible access points. Their meaning grows when connected to lore, games, factions, and identity.

5. Street Culture Roots

Before wallets, there were walls. Before NFTs, there were names. Before tokens, there were crews. Crypto Moonboys comes from the Graffiti Kings / GKniftyHEADS world: graffiti, street art, outreach, youth creativity, events, music, public culture, and permissionless marks.
