Battle Chamber — The Proof Wall

The Battle Chamber is where Crypto Moonboys activity becomes visible. If the wiki is memory and the arcade is action, the Battle Chamber is proof. It shows who is moving, who is playing, who is syncing, who is building position, and which factions are carrying signal.

1. What The Battle Chamber Is

This is not a normal community page. It is the public proof wall of the living Web3 wiki. The Battle Chamber turns invisible activity into visible movement.

It should show:

  • Arcade XP movement
  • leaderboard pressure
  • live activity
  • faction alignment
  • mission movement
  • Telegram-linked identity
  • next steps into Block Topia

2. Why It Matters

People believe what they can see. A leaderboard, activity feed, faction signal, and progression panel turn the system from “trust us” into “look”. The Battle Chamber is where action becomes public.

3. Join The Arcade War

Play games. Earn Arcade XP. Complete missions. Join a faction. Help your side move. Seasons and deeper faction wars may expand over time, but the behaviour starts now: show up, act, sync, and be seen.

4. Factions Are Crews

Factions are not just labels. They are the digital crew layer. Faction choice affects supported arcade games through in-run perks, upgrade bias, and faction signal. Faction effects apply where supported.

Diamond Hands: Best for long survival and clean play. Perks favour endurance, late-game scoring, and reduced chaos pressure.
HODL Warriors: Best for defense and safer runs. Perks favour starting shields, mistake recovery, and defensive upgrades.
GraffPUNKS: Best for chaos, combos, and high-risk score bursts. Perks favour rare spawns, combo multipliers, and chaotic event frequency.

Faction signal updates after accepted activity. Submit score to qualify for Arcade XP. Accepted runs can sync Arcade XP when Telegram is linked.

5. What Is Live Now

  • activity panels
  • leaderboard / contributor surfaces
  • Telegram identity status
  • faction alignment
  • arcade links
  • mission board surfaces where implemented
  • Battle Chamber as public proof layer

6. What Comes Later

  • full seasonal HODL Wars
  • deeper faction standings
  • richer mission layers
  • more public unlock feeds
  • stronger Block Topia connection
  • creator and builder signals
