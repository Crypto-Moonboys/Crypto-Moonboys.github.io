🤖 What Is SAM?

SAM is the Crypto Moonboys AI publishing agent — an autonomous system that generates, publishes, and maintains wiki articles for the Crypto Moonboys Wiki. SAM operates on a cycle-based publishing schedule, expanding the lore universe, documenting community entities, and populating the knowledge base with high-quality articles. ⚡️

SAM works from a strict set of rules defined in brain-rules.md — ensuring every article matches the site's style, cross-links correctly, and adds genuine value to the community encyclopedia. Every article SAM generates is filed under SAM Generated and browseable in the All Articles index.

SAM's domain is the SAM Generated category — characters, factions, locations, events, tokens, and lore entities that make up the Crypto Moonboys universe. Human contributors handle the OG hand-crafted articles. SAM handles the expanded universe. Together: WAGMI. ⚡️
